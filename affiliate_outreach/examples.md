# Affiliate Outreach — Examples

## Example 1: Newsletter operator, strong fit

**Product + terms:** "$197 SaaS billing course. 30% commission, 60-day cookie, $59 avg payout."

**Prospect:** "@maria runs the 'Indie Stripe' newsletter, 18k subs, weekly issue, mostly indie SaaS founders."

**Output highlights:**
- fit_score: 9
- email_body opens with reference to her last issue's topic (not generic "love your newsletter")
- States 30% / 60-day / $59 avg explicitly
- Ask: free copy + 15 min if interested
- research_gaps: "verify she takes affiliates at all"

## Example 2: Podcast host, soft fit

**Prospect:** "Tony hosts a podcast about productivity, 3k weekly downloads, broad audience including some SaaS founders but mostly knowledge workers."

**Output highlights:**
- fit_score: 5
- best_angle: "the SaaS billing audience is a slice — pitch a niche episode, not a broad endorsement"
- email_body proposes a specific episode angle, not a "promote my course" ask
- do_not_pursue_signals: empty (worth a try) but research_gaps notes audience overlap is the risk

## Example 3: Wrong fit

**Prospect:** "Lisa runs a knitting blog, 500 subscribers."

**Output highlights:**
- fit_score: 1
- do_not_pursue_signals: ["audience mismatch — knitting subscribers are not SaaS founders", "list size below useful threshold"]
- email_body still generated for completeness but flagged not worth sending
